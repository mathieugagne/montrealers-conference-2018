### Building a grain bill

Malt is a function.

- Gives sugar
- Gives mouthfeel
- Gives colour
- Gives enzymes
- Gives flavour
- Gives head retention

Metallic taste can come from humidity in the grain.
Higher than 9% humidity, enzymes start working on your grain and give a metallic taste.
